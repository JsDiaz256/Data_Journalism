Food Availability in New York City

Description of project:

This project will compare the amount of farmers markets, food carts, snack bars, and restaurants there are in different boroughs and zip codes in NYC. While this excludes other types of food stores, like fast food chains or coffee shops, this project shows a general trend of where there are the most businesses to grab a bite to eat in NYC.

Vocabulary:

  • NYC - abbreviation for New York City
  • Borough - one of five major areas of NYC, a way to differentiate the parts of the islands
  • Businesses or eateries - generic term to encompass all of the types of food-selling shops listed above
